Ingredients

  • 6 Serves Pasta
  • 1kg Beef mince
  • 2 Cans Diced Tomatoes
  • 1 Large Brown Onion
    •

    Steps

    1. Dice onion
    2. Head oil in large pan and bring some salted water to boil
    3. Place onions in the pan. Once the onions are brown, add beef mince
    4. Place pasta in the pot
    5. Once beef is browned, add diced tomatoes, salt and herbs for flavour. Allow to simmer
    6. Once pasta is aldente, drain
    7. Place sauce on pasta in bowl and serve
