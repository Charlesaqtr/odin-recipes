Oatshake: A new way?

Okay, so picture this: the most luxurious, indulgent oat milkshake you've ever laid your eyes on. It's like a work of art, darlings. Creamy, dreamy, and totally guilt-free. When I sip on my oat milkshake, I feel like a true goddess, ready to conquer the world.

You see, oat milk is all the rage right now, and for a good reason. It's plant-based, dairy-free, and incredibly good for you. But trust me, my oat milkshake takes it to a whole new level. It's a blend of velvety oats, sweet vanilla, and a hint of cinnamon. Absolutely divine.

I top it off with a sprinkle of gold flakes because, well, I'm all about that glam life. It's a treat for your taste buds and your Instagram feed. One sip and you'll be hooked, just like I am. So whether you're a health-conscious babe or just want to indulge in something fabulous, my oat milkshake is an absolute must-try. It's like liquid gold, beauties!

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Milk
  • Gold flake
  • Some hyper-processed whey thick with sucralose
    •

    Steps

    1. Put milk in blender
    2. Put oat in blender
    3. Put whey in blender
    4. Blend
    5. Pour into cup, serve with gold flakes
